SDSS DR5 Quasar data and random catalogues

In /data

Use of the SDSS DR5 Quasar catalogue requires reference to Schneider et al., 2007, AJ, 134, 102.
Use of the SDSS DR5 Quasar UNIFORM sample (including using the bolometric luminosity or virial BH mass information) requires reference to Shen et al. 2008, ApJ, 680, 169.

DATA

dr5qso.fits (22Mb) is the full, 77,429 object strong DR5 Quasar Catalogue from Schneider et al., (2007).
DR5QSO_uni_data.fits (21Mb) is the 'UNIFORM' quasar sample. The criteria for this sample originate in Richards et al. 2006, AJ, 131, 2766 and also in Shen et al., 2007, AJ, 133, 2222.

The UNIFORM sample itself was used in our sister paper, Shen et al. (2009, ApJ accepted, arXiv:0810.4144v2).

For ease, the data file that is read into the FORTRAN 2 Point Correlation Function code is: DR5QSO_uni_data.dat (2.4Mb).

DR5QSO_uni_data_wFibre.dat (2.4Mb) and DR5QSO_uni_data_wFibre_v2.dat (2.4Mb) are the two data files that are used to check for the effects of fibre collisions in Appendix C.
DR5QSO_uni_data_EBV_le_0pnt0217.dat (1.2Mb) and DDR5QSO_uni_data_EBV_gt_0pnt0217.dat (1.2Mb) are the two data files that are used to check for the effects of dust reddening in Appendix C.

Randoms

The necessary random catalogue files are: randoms_npr_UNIFORM.dat (62Mb).
randoms_npr_UNIFORM_EBV_gt_0pnt0217.dat (32Mb) and randoms_npr_UNIFORM_EBV_le_0pnt0217.dat (31Mb) needed for the Reddening checks (Appendix C.5).

FORTRAN 90 Two Point Correlation Function code
In /src directory.

quorrel_five_perl.f90

This code is fairly well commented but a bit "clunky".
Needs two input files, the data and the random catalogues from above.
This is the main program that takes SDSS Quasar data (DR5) and computes:
- the redshift-space correlation function, xi(s),
- the ``2-D'' correlation function, xi(sigma,pi)
- the projected correlation function, wp(rp).

IDL Scripts
In the /pro directory.
Note, currently only the xi(s), wp(rp) and the bias evolution files and routines are here (but please feel free to email me if something not present is required).

xi(s)
In /pro/xis
xis_plot.pro does the work here.
k_output_UNI22.dat (6.6k)is the xi(s) measurement for the complete UNIFORM sample.
While k_output_UNI22_XpntXXzYpntYY_10log.dat (~5k) are the redshift slice files over X.XX < z < Y.YY.
k_output_UNI22_jackknife_errors.dat has the jackknife errors.

The 2QZ data was kindly supplied by S.M. Croom.
Any use of this data requires citation of Croom et al., 2005, MRNAS, 356, 415).

Any use of the results from the 2SLAQ QSO Survey requires citation of da Angela et al., 2008, MRNAS, 383, 565).

wp(rp)
In /pro/wp_div_rp
K_wp_output_UNI22.dat (1.4k) is the wp(rp) measurement for the complete UNIFORM sample.
